Alex Ovechkin Goal Projection

This is a simple projection system that combines recent shot rates, medium term time on ice averages and long term shooting percentages in both even strength and power play situations to attempt to determine how many goals Alex Ovechkin will score in the current season. In addition to those factors, it also combines those same statistics with their current season analogs on a weighted basis.
